Salaty is a beautiful and comprehensive Islamic prayer app designed to help Muslims maintain their daily prayers.

Features:
• Accurate prayer times based on your location
• Multiple calculation methods (Muslim World League, ISNA, Egypt, etc.)
• Prayer tracking - mark prayers as completed
• Qibla compass to find the direction of Mecca
• Home screen widgets (compact & full)
• Prayer notifications with customizable sounds
• Statistics and streaks to track your progress
• Qada (makeup prayers) management
• Support for both Shafi and Hanafi madhab
• Dark mode support
• Available in English and Arabic

Privacy-focused:
• No ads
• No tracking
• Location used only for prayer time calculations
• All data stored locally on your device

Open source and free forever.
