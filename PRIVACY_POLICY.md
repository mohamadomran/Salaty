# Privacy Policy for Salaty

**Last Updated: November 6, 2024**

## Introduction

Salaty ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Salaty mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **Purpose**: We collect your location data to calculate accurate Islamic prayer times based on your geographic coordinates
- **Usage**: Location data is used locally on your device and is NOT transmitted to any external servers
- **Storage**: Location coordinates are stored locally on your device for convenience
- **Permissions Required**: ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION, ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION

### Local Storage
- **Prayer Tracking Data**: Your prayer completion records and statistics are stored locally on your device
- **Settings & Preferences**: App settings, calculation methods, and notification preferences are stored locally
- **No Cloud Sync**: All data remains on your device and is not synchronized to any cloud services

### Notifications
- **Purpose**: We use notification permissions to remind you of prayer times
- **Usage**: Notifications are generated locally on your device based on calculated prayer times
- **Control**: You can enable/disable notifications at any time in the app settings
- **Permission Required**: POST_NOTIFICATIONS

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:
1. Calculate accurate prayer times based on your location
2. Display Qibla direction for prayer
3. Send prayer time notifications
4. Track your prayer completion (stored locally)
5. Provide statistical insights on your prayer habits

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

**We do NOT:**
- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your personal information
- Upload your data to external servers
- Track your activity outside the app
- Use analytics or advertising services

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device
- No internet connection is required for core app functionality
- Data is protected by your device's built-in security features
- You can delete all app data by uninstalling the application

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (all stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data (by clearing app data or uninstalling)
- Disable location services at any time
- Disable notifications at any time
- Use the app offline

## Children's Privacy

Salaty does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for all ages and does not collect or transmit personal data.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Location Data Usage Details

### Why We Need Location
Islamic prayer times are calculated based on the position of the sun relative to your location. Accurate coordinates are essential for correct prayer time calculations.

### Background Location
Background location access allows the app to:
- Update prayer times automatically when you move to a new location
- Send timely prayer notifications based on your current position
- Function properly when running in the background

You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings, though this will affect prayer time accuracy.

## Data Retention

- Prayer tracking data: Retained indefinitely on your device until you delete it
- Location data: Updated when you manually refresh or move to a new location
- Settings: Retained until you reset them or uninstall the app
- All data can be cleared through app settings or by uninstalling
