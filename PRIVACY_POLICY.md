# Privacy Policy for Salaty

**Last updated: December 2024**

## Overview

Salaty ("the App") is an open-source Islamic prayer times application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Location data**: Used solely to calculate accurate prayer times for your area. Location is processed on-device and is not transmitted to any server.

### What We Don't Collect
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Advertising identifiers
- Any data for third-party sharing

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:
- Prayer records and tracking history
- App preferences and settings
- Saved locations

No data is transmitted to external servers. The App works entirely offline after initial setup.

## Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:
- **Location**: To calculate prayer times based on your geographical position
- **Notifications**: To alert you when prayer times arrive
- **Internet**: Only used for reverse geocoding (converting coordinates to city names)

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Open Source

Salaty is open source. You can review the complete source code at:
https://github.com/mohamad/SalatyAndroid

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.

## Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

---

This privacy policy applies to the Salaty Android application.
